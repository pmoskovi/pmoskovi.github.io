Why Don't You Have Honey On the Stand?

Having our honey out on the stand and offering our neighbors a no-touch self-serve experience was our first choice and it worked incredibly well. Unfortunately on several occasions the items disappeared from the stand.

To be clear, we have no problem gifting honey and bee products to anybody, including those, who cannot pay for them. Feel free to take a jar, and ejoy it. But when all the items are taken from the stand, then it makes this model of sharing our joy with you all unsustainable. We truly regret that we have to make this change, becuase the feedback we have been receiving has been unanomously positive and your support means the world to us.
