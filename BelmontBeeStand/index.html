About our bees and honey

It is our passion to help the threatened honeybees. We care for our bees responsibly, humanly, and sustainably. We don’t treat them with chemicals, and don’t take them to the almond orchards for pollination.

We extract and bottle our honey by hand, frame by frame, ensuring the richest flavor in every jar.

Comb honey

Our comb honey comes directly from the beehive the way the bees built it and filled it with honey.

Flavor: delicate spring blooms lend this honey a light, clean, sweet flavor.

Safety: honeycomb is perfectly safe and delicious to consume. Some like to swallow the chewy textured comb, others enjoy it as gum.

Our favorite and recommended ways to consume comb honey:

  • Comb honey & goat cheese – appetizer
  • Comb honey on toast – breakfast
  • Comb honey & chocolate – sweets for the win
  • Comb honey on its own – for the purists
